Activity

EDUCATIONAL STIPENDS

In case of stipends up to the level of Madhyamik, willing students are required to contact the local centre Secretaries for the procedure to follow and in case of Higher Studies stipends, students are advised to contact the General Secretary at the Main Centre. During the year 2011-12 three hundred fifty students up to the level of Madhyamik and one hundred more in Higher Studies have been granted educational stipends. All stipends are disbursed through local centers and main centre in due time.

LIBRARIES

Four libraries are running successfully in different areas under the supervision of local centres. Books under Educational syllabus along with all other types of books are made available in the libraries. Regular up gradation of books in list are done as demands arrive from the students end with direct financial help from the main centre. Presently following libraries are functioning-


Aurobindo Rita Memorial Library at 445, Rabindra Sarani, Sobazar, Kolkata-70005. For this we are indebted to Jyotsna Daw & Jyotirmoy Daw. Well maintained by the Sovabazar Local Centre this library has a different Children Section enriched with all sorts of children books and magazines. It is open to all on every Sunday from 10am to 12 noon.

Pathachakra, the oldest library run by the Society now functioning properly under Pataldanga Local Centre at 7A, Sitaram Ghosh Street Kolkata-700009, residence of Sri Ajit Paul & Srimati Bani Paul. The Society is indebted to them for the same. The library opens at 10am and continues up to 12 noon on every Sunday.

Kalikrishna Mitra Memorial Library, one of the oldest library in Bardhaman city is now functioning well under Bardhaman Local Centre. Enthused members organize various other programmes in the library like Vocational Training for Women, Computer Courses for all, Coaching Class, Drawing Class and a Health Centre. These made the library most popular in Bardhaman town. /p>

VOCATIONAL TRAINING (TAILORING SCHOOL)

A Gandhabanik Education Society initiative the Rita Memorial Tailoring School was founded to train the women of the society who needs it to support their family. On many occasions, the handcrafted materials of this school gathered applaud for their intricacy. The school runs at 445 Rabindra Sarani Kolkata-5 under Sovabazar Local Centre. Arrangements are made to promote the trainees after successful completion of their courses. Exhibition cum Sale programmes are organised occasionally. Apart from this the Society provides sewing machines to the needy persons to earn for their own bread and butter.

PUBLICATION

"Unmesh" a magazine for all section of the society, published on Annual Outing. Plans are there to publish it monthly. All sorts of articles are invited. Please contact your local centre Secretary to submit articles. Creative ideas and constructive opinions are invited for the betterment of this magazine.

HEALTH CENTRES

Two health centres are running well and serving the backward section of the society with medical treatment and medicines as per availability. One is functioning under Shibpur Local Centre at Kedarnath Pathsala Bhawan, Nabagopal Mukherjee Lane , Shibpur Howrah on every Thursday in the evening. Another is in Bardhaman District at Kalikrishna Mitra Memorial Library at Bardhaman town under Bardhaman Local Centre. It is meant for all section of the society and functions on every Tuesday & Saturday from 5pm to 7pm.

VARIOUS PROGRAMMES

ANNUAL OUTING

Every year Gandhabanik Education Society organizes Annual Educational Outing where merit prizes are distributed to the successful students of all level of education along with various sports events. Exhibition cum sale counters are also arranged for the handicraft items of the tailoring school. Day by day this Outing is taking the shape of a carnival. Now a day approximately some 5000 participants join the day long programme.

FOUNDATION DAY CELEBRATION

Foundation Day of the Society falls in end of the month April. Every year a programme is organised to commemorate the day on the last Sunday of the month April where successful students of higher studies are felicitated followed by various cultural programmes staged by different local centres.

DEBATE COMPETITION

This is also a student guardian oriented programme where students and guardians from all local centres participate and indulge themselves in competitive debate over various motions fixed by the Society. The programme gives the student a great opportunity to become stage free which helps them to perform better in their competitive life.

MUSIC COMPETITION

For last Nine years the music competition in vocal stream is organised under Sovabazar local centre. Mainly Rabindrasangeet is the medium. There are five categories depending on the age of the competitors. The programme is very attractive and popular amongst the members of the society.

QUIZ CONTEST

A Pataldanga local centre initiative and fully backed by the main centre this programme is one of the most popular programmes of the Society. This is completely a student oriented programme where students from all local centres participate in group to contest with each other. The students thus get chance to enrich their knowledge bank.

VIJAYA SAMMILANI

A fraternal get together programme to celebrate the auspicious Durga Puja days. On this occasion the Society arranges to felicitate the meritorious students of Secondary & Higher Secondary Examination of that year followed by various cultural programmes staged by different local centres.
