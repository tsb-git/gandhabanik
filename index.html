History

Our History

When pecuniary doldrums force a student to leave his studies, pain and frustration engulfs the parents first, a sheer silent cry outbreaks in their hearts for the incapability of funding their own subject’s prosperous future. Gandhabanik Education Society has been trying it’s best to give those frustrated parents as well as the students a little hand to fight against those odds. Gandhabanik education Society has ten(10) local centres in Sovabazar, Baranagar, Chorbagan, Pataldanga, Kidderpore and Bhowanipur local centres in Kolkata district, Rishra local centre in Hooghly district, Shibpur and Khantora local centre in Howrah district and Bardhaman local centre in Bardhaman district to cater it’s services and to keep close contact with the members and students of the community to grow better relationship.
